Rosalind Franklin

Discover Rosalind Franklin, a pioneer of DNA structure whose essential role was long overshadowed. Through the popularization of her thesis, this site makes her research and the scientific methods that revealed the double helix accessible. The associated film rehabilitates her figure, showing her struggle and fundamental contribution to science while bringing her story to life. Finally, the document from the university library concretely illustrates her work and serves as an educational tool, placing Franklin at the heart of scientific history. Together, these resources combine narrative, analysis, and archives to correct the injustice of the invisibility of women in science and give Rosalind Franklin the recognition she deserves.
